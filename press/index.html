Short description

Explain Yourself is a fast mobile icebreaker game built around ridiculous prompts and even more ridiculous answers.

Long description

Explain Yourself turns awkward pauses into instant party energy. Each round pushes players to defend bizarre answers under pressure, creating a loop that is fast to start, easy to replay, and perfect for casual groups, game nights, and late-night chaos.

Feature bullets

  • Fast rounds with minimal setup
  • No account required
  • Designed for two players or full groups
  • Privacy-forward, on-device experience
  • Built for repeatable party sessions
