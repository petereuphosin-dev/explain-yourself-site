Privacy

Privacy policy

Explain Yourself is designed as a simple consumer game experience. No account is required to use the app, and the default expectation is that gameplay data stays on your device.

What we collect

This website stores local preferences such as dark mode and cookie consent. The app itself is intended to work without collecting personal profile data, and no account creation is required.

How your data is used

Preferences stored by the website are used only to remember your display choices on this device. The app is positioned so that prompts, round state, and local settings remain on-device unless you later add a clearly disclosed feature that changes that behavior.

Sales and sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not market this app as a data brokerage or ad-targeting product.

Your choices

  • You can clear local website cookies in your browser at any time.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.
  • You can contact support if you have questions about future updates or privacy changes.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@peuphosin.com.

Cookie preferences

We only use essential cookies for preferences like dark mode.
